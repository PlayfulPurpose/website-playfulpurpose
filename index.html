About Julie

Serious about play.

I'm a fractional Chief Play Officer, workshop designer, and speaker based in Sydney.

I've spent 30 years at the intersection of people, learning, and technology - from inner-city classrooms in Atlanta to the Salesforce Foundation, Workday Foundation, and Telstra's muru-D accelerator. I've helped build programs that scaled globally and worked with founders, executives, and teams across three continents.

What I kept seeing everywhere: the human stuff is always the hard stuff. Communication, trust, conflict, connection. No amount of strategy or technology fixes a team that can't work together.

Play is my methodology. Applied Improvisation is my toolkit. And I'm serious about both.
